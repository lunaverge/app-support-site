常见问题

来电不显示 / 没有反应？

请确认已在「设置 → 通知」中允许本 App 的通知权限；首次使用建议先添加一位“来电方”。

定时来电到点没响？

iOS 在后台会限制应用运行，到点时会以通知形式提醒，点击通知即可进入来电界面。请确保已允许通知权限。

贴脸熄屏不生效？

通话中靠近听筒上方的距离传感器即可熄屏，移开自动亮起，与真实通话一致。

如何切换语言 / 深浅色？

在「设置 → 外观」中可切换主题（跟随系统/浅色/深色）与语言（跟随系统/中文/English）。

我的数据安全吗？

所有数据仅保存在你的设备本地，不上传、不收集。详见 隐私政策。

FAQ

The call doesn't appear / nothing happens?

Make sure notifications are allowed under Settings → Notifications. On first use, add a “caller” first.

A scheduled call didn't ring on time?

iOS limits background activity, so at the scheduled time you'll get a notification — tap it to open the call screen. Please keep notifications enabled.

Proximity screen-off isn't working?

During a call, cover the proximity sensor near the earpiece to turn the screen off; move away to wake it — just like a real call.

How do I switch language / appearance?

Under Settings → Appearance you can switch theme (System/Light/Dark) and language (System/中文/English).

Is my data safe?

All data stays on your device and is never uploaded or collected. See our Privacy Policy for details.
