「模拟电话」是一款娱乐用途的模拟来电工具。我们高度重视你的隐私，并坚持最小化、本地化的数据原则。

1. 我们收集哪些信息

我们不收集、不上传、不出售你的任何个人信息。本 App 没有服务器、不联网传输任何用户数据，也不包含任何第三方分析或广告 SDK。

2. App 访问的设备数据

为实现功能，App 可能在你授权后访问以下内容，且这些数据仅存储在你的设备本地，绝不离开设备：

你随时可以在系统「设置」中撤销以上任一权限。

你创建的来电方、头像、偏好设置等均保存在 App 的本地存储中。卸载 App 即会删除全部相关数据。

本 App 不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的任何信息。

“Simulated Call” is an entertainment app for simulating incoming phone calls. We take your privacy seriously and follow a strict data-minimization, on-device-only principle.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal information. The app has no server, sends no user data over the network, and contains no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

2. Device Data the App Accesses

To provide its features, the app may access the following with your permission. This data stays entirely on your device and never leaves it:

  • Photos / Camera: to set an avatar for a caller.
  • Notifications: to alert you of a scheduled simulated call.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time in your system Settings.

3. Data Storage & Deletion

Callers, avatars, and preferences you create are stored only in the app's local storage. Deleting the app removes all associated data.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
